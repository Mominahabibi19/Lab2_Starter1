New Business Discussion

In our upcoming Arduino Workshop, participants will have the opportunity to delve into the basics of microcontroller programming and electronic circuit design. The workshop will be led by Dr. Ali Rohan, an expert in robotic systems. Participants will engage in hands-on activities designed to demonstrate how Arduino can be used to create interactive projects and automation solutions.

In conclusion, the board emphasizes the urgency of revising our current curriculum to include more practical, hands-on experiences. These updates are crucial to maintaining our accreditation and attracting prospective students.
